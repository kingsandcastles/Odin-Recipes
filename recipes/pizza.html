Homemade Margherita Pizza

Pizza really is a universal language.

Yea, we are getting pretty cheesy in here, but look at the tomato field. Many pizza are great, and many are trash. What I am really getting at is an appreciation of quality. May it not be for the greatest of chefs to accomplish a simple task, but for them to do it of a high standard.

Ingredients

  • 1 premade pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups sliced mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/8 cup fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475F and start your mise en place for your pizza.
  2. On a floured surface, roll and toss your dough into a proper pizza shape.
  3. Spread your pizza sauce, layer your parmesan cheese, and place your mozzarella cheese pieces.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes until the crust is lightly browned. Don't burn your pizza.
  5. Gently and lightly layer the leaves over your finished pizza.
